Who We Are

A global name,
built for Indonesian courts.

Tienda PadelPoint Indonesia is part of a globally established brand recognized across the padel world — with stores in Spain, Portugal, Italy, France, Mexico, and now Indonesia. One standard of quality and expertise, in every market we touch.

As the world's No.1 padel shop, we carefully select every racket, apparel piece, shoe, bag, and accessory to meet players where they are — first match or competition court.

Driven by authenticity, quality, and passion for the sport, Tienda Padelpoint continues to be a trusted destination for the global padel community.

Vision

To become the leading padel and tennis retail and distribution group in Southeast Asia.

Mission

To grow padel and tennis in Indonesia through authentic products, expert service, and a premium retail experience for every player.
